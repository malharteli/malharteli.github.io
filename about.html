About Malhar Teli

Biography

Malhar Teli is the son of Prakash and Veena Teli, both of whom immigrated from Gujurat India and obtained Masters Degress in the States. Both have extensive background in Computer Science and take an active role in their son's education. The boy was raised in Sharon Massachusetts, a small town located about half-an-hour south from the city of Boston. Growing up, Malhar was constantly seeking to involve himself in the community. By the time he graduated, Malhar Teli had become the Senior Facilitator of an Interfaith Community initiative called Interfaith Youth Youth Action. When he had enrolled into Northeastern University, Malhar had originally planned to follow a Marketing career, but found his calling in Game Design thanks in part to his experiences with the staff of the Northeastern Game Design program. Since he transferred, Malhar has gotten involved with several small projects and is constantly looking to get his hands dirty with a new game project.
