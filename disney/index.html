Drawings & Sketches

  • Crazy Sheep

    This sheep started leering at me while I was drawing it. I had to adjust the drawing accordingly. This digital version was done with a brush mimicking water colors

  • Frog Studies

    One of the exercises I had to do for a course was to draw an animal at "work." The image that resulted from this visit to the Harvard Natural History museum will appear later.

  • King Cobra

    I like snakes. They have strange and unique behaviors that take full advantage of a body that is effectively one giant spine. Saw this one in the Franklin Park Zoo.

  • Yawning Tiger

    Everybody loves tigers. This was the moment when I understood why- a yawning tiger is adorable.

  • Salamander

    I almost stepped on this little guy in Orlando, Florida. It just stood there, long enough for me to quickly sketch the shape into my sketchbook.

  • Tortoise

    All my life, I've wanted to see a Galapogos Tortoise. Sadly, this drawing is of Boxy, a smaller kind of Terrapin

  • Wing Study

    Wings are awesome. Finding out how they work was even more awesome. Learning how to draw a wing based on that anatomy was superb.

  • Big Dog

    In case you were wondering, I normally do a lot of post-processing on these drawings. In some cases, I even redraw them. This image is a raw scan of a sketch from my book.

  • Squirrel

    Squirrels, while common, have silhouettes made mostly of fur. This proves to be a challenge for me, resulting in this piece

  • Torso Antique
